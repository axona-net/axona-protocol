@axona/protocol — single-peer pub/sub demo

One Axona peer in this browser tab — both publisher and subscriber on us-east/hello-world. Pick a name, type a message, hit Publish. The signed envelope routes through the axonal pub/sub tree (this peer is the K-closest replica for the topic) and arrives back at your own subscriber. Open more tabs once a real transport is wired in and they'll join the same topic as additional peers.
