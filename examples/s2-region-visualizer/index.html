What you are seeing

Every Axona nodeId starts with an 8-bit S2 prefix derived from the peer's lat/lng via a Hilbert curve over a 16×16 grid. The prefix anchors pub/sub topics: K-closest routing for a publisher-keyed topic naturally finds peers in the publisher's region.

Cells are uniform 11.25° × 22.5° rectangles in equirectangular space — which means they shrink toward the poles in ground distance. The polar cells cover much less surface area than the equatorial ones. This is a known property of flat lat/lng partitioning.
